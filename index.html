What Awaits You?

Hands on Workshops

Sharpen your technical skills through four diverse and hands-on workshops, each tailored to help you gain practical knowledge in cutting-edge technologies. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced learner, there's something for everyone to explore and master.

Cultural Eve

Immerse yourself in an unforgettable evening of cultural performances, music, and celebrations. Join us as we showcase diverse talents and traditions, bringing everyone together to relax, enjoy, and connect through the beauty of culture.

Panel Discussion

Engage with industry leaders and experts in thought-provoking discussions on the latest trends and challenges in the field. This is your opportunity to gain valuable insights and perspectives from professionals at the forefront of innovation.

Technical Talks

Listen to experts as they delve into cutting-edge technologies and emerging trends in the industry. These sessions are designed to inspire and provide valuable knowledge that can help you stay ahead in the ever-evolving tech landscape.

Chairs Meet

Join us for the Chairs Meet, where Student Branch Chairs from the Travancore Hub gather to share their activities, exchange ideas, and strengthen connections. This session fosters collaboration, helping elevate the impact of IEEE across the region.

Networking

Connect with like-minded peers, industry professionals, and experts during dedicated networking sessions. Build meaningful relationships, exchange ideas, and explore potential collaborations in a relaxed and engaging environment.

About Us

THM'24

THM is a hallmark event organised by IEEE Travancore Hub, set against the backdrop of cutting-edge technology and vibrant community interaction. This flagship event is designed to inspire and empower students under the IEEE Travancore Hub. Known for its blend of technical excellence and community spirit, THM brings together over 120 participants, serving as a key platform for innovation, skill development, and networking.


About Host

The IEEE Student Branch at TKM College of Engineering (IEEE SB TKMCE), established in 1986, is a prominent technical community in Kerala. IEEE SB TKMCIt serves as a platform for students to engage in various technical and professional development activities, including workshops, seminars, competitions, and projects, fostering innovation and learning among its members.
